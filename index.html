Our Spaces & Projects

We use our physical network and strong cultural position to share ground-level music culture with the masses. Our spaces are the foundation of our community.

Physical Spaces

  • Next Door Records Shepherds Bush: Record shop, venue and community hangout.
  • When I Met You Studios: Outboard recording studio with a long list of world-class hardware.
  • Next Door Records Two: Record shop/music venue and community hangout.
  • New Forms: World Class Audio focussed cocktail bar, and daytime hangout.

Our Position in the Market

Market Gap

The connections that record shops have to grass roots, emerging & established artists are obvious & unmatched. However, times have changed. Shopping and socialising trends have changed - and we know that connectivity is limited solely through retail. Using our additional foundation of consistent events programmes & our social presence we believe that our brand can fill a gap in the modern digital music landscape.

Physical Engagement

We strongly believe that there are few competitors with the physical engagement we have pushing into the digital market properly too. Rough Trade have proved that the physical record shop / record label is a strong model. We have direct relationships to almost ALL record shops in the UK. This is another direct ground up marketing strategy since we have already gained respect from the curators - we are able to gather attention where it matters.
